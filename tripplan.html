PLANNING YOUR TRIP

TICKETS

To start your journey into the stars, go to the "Purchasing a Trip" page in the navigation menu above. You are able to reserve one seat on our advanced ship for $450,000. Since we have such few spots per flight and such a long waiting list, we handle your ticket purchases through our email found on the "Purchasing a Trip" page.

TRAINING

Because you will be transported to the limits of space through a one of a kind space-plane, there will be minimal training to withstand the G-force. Majority of the training entailed is concerned with familiarizing yourself with emergency procedures, getting adjusted to sensory overload, and making sure that you are fit enough for the intense conditions of the flight.

BLAST OFF

Once your training is done, get ready to reach new horizons by showing up to our launch site with your ticket on the monthly launch date that you registered for. Be prepared to witness the beauty of the planet we live on while more than 100 kilometers above ground, as only handfuls of people have before.

LANDING

On your way down from space, the ship's rudder will reconfigure into a "gliding" mode, where it will slow down and descend until it reaches the runway from which you took off, then deploying its landing gear and activating its brakes, giving you a safe and smooth delivery back to earth.
