PLANNING YOUR TRIP

TICKETS

To start your journey into the stars, go to the "Purchasing a Trip" page in the navigation menu above. You are able to reserve one seat on our advanced ship for $450,000.

TRAINING

Because you will be transported to the limits of space through a one of a kind space-plane, there will be minimal training to withstand the G-force. Majority of the training entailed is concerned with familiarizing yourself with emergency procedures, getting adjusted to sensory overload, and making sure that you are fit enough for the intense conditions of the flight.
