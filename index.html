ABOUT US

Here at GreenScene, we are dedicated to leveraging our expertise in sustainable living and cutting-edge technology to guide homeowners towards a greener future. Our mission is to empower homeowners with the knowledge and tools they need to embrace green and clean energy home solutions while promoting the widespread adoption of renewable energy.

The SpacePlaneTwo is our state of the art space vehicle made to take you to the lower thermosphere. It takes off of the runway with the help of two carrier planes (see the picture on the right) which will release it once they reach the proper altitude. Once released, the SpacePlaneTwo will use its hybrid rocket engine to propel itself into outer space, and to then re-enter into the atmosphere, at which point it will adjust rudder formation and glide down to the runway and smoothly land.
