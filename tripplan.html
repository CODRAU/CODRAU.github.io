SAVING ENERGY

EFFICIENCY

To use less power, first check your house for spots to fix. Put money in green tools like cold boxes and cleaning machines to cut down on energy over time. Make sure your house keeps heat well and has no cold leaks to keep it warm or cool without too much use of warmers or cool airs. Move to LED bulbs to save energy and they shine for more time. A clever heat control lets you set the heat just right, changing it if people are there or not. Doing these things drops your costs and helps the Earth, making a lasting good for everyone.

RENEWABILITY

Sun panels, wind mills, and earth heat setups are clean power types that boost eco-friendly power and save energy. Sun panels catch sun rays, wind mills turn wind into power, and earth heat setups use warmth from the ground, all cutting down on the need for old fuel and dropping gas that warms the planet. By using these endless sources, we can push for a more earth-kind and lasting tomorrow.

LANDSCAPING

Planting trees and shrubs for shade helps improve air quality. Trees and shrubs provide natural shading, reducing the need for artificial cooling systems during hot weather, and consequently lowering energy consumption. Additionally, they absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, helping to slow climate change and improve air quality. By enhancing natural cooling and air purification, planting trees and shrubs supports a more sustainable environment while reducing our carbon footprint.

ENERGY PRICING

The cost of solar panel installation differs heavily based on state and residence size, but here are some quick stats to decide what solar system to get based on your energy needs and budget: A 6kW system costs between $10,200 - $15,200 after Federal Tax Credit, and produces between 8000-10000 kWh. An 8kW system costs from $13,600 - $20,300 After Federal Tax Credit and produces 10,500 - 13,300 kWh. A 10kW system costs $17,000 - $25,300 After Federal Tax Credit and produces 13,300 - 16,700 kWh. Finally, a 12kW system costs $20,400 - $30,500 After Federal Tax Credit and produces 16,000 - 20,000 kWh.
