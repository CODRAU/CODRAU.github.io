SAVING ENERGY

EFFICIENCY

To use less power, first check your house for spots to fix. Put money in green tools like cold boxes and cleaning machines to cut down on energy over time. Make sure your house keeps heat well and has no cold leaks to keep it warm or cool without too much use of warmers or cool airs. Move to LED bulbs to save energy and they shine for more time. A clever heat control lets you set the heat just right, changing it if people are there or not. Doing these things drops your costs and helps the Earth, making a lasting good for everyone.

RENEWABILITY

Sun panels, wind mills, and earth heat setups are clean power types that boost eco-friendly power and save energy. Sun panels catch sun rays, wind mills turn wind into power, and earth heat setups use warmth from the ground, all cutting down on the need for old fuel and dropping gas that warms the planet. By using these endless sources, we can push for a more earth-kind and lasting tomorrow.

LANDING

On your way down from space, the ship's rudder will reconfigure into a "gliding" mode, where it will slow down and descend until it reaches the runway from which you took off, then deploying its landing gear and activating its brakes, giving you a safe and smooth delivery back to earth.
